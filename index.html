Moon Crystal Power Play

* You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

* There are four crystal brooches below. By clicking on a brooch you will add a specific amount of points to your Crystal Value.

* You win the game by matching your Crystal Value to the Match Value, you lose the game if your total score goes above the Match Value.

* The value of each crystal brooch is hidden from you until you click on it.

* Each time the game re-starts, the game will change the values of each crystal brooch.
